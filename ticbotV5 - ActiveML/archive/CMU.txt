Modelação e animação 3d - Workshop 360º (Grátis e Limitado a 10 participantes)
Realiza-se sexta-feira, 12 de abril, um workshop em filmagem 360º, ministrado pela Empresa Creative Lemons. O workshop é gratuito e limitado a 10 alunos do curso que deverão trazer um computador com o Adobe Premiere e o Adobe Photoshop Adobe Audition instalados.
<br />
<br />Os interessados deverão enviar um email para jjsousa@utad.pt. SÓ OS PRIMEIROS 10 TERÃO LUGAR NO WORKSHOP, FICANDO OS RESTANTES COMO SUPLENTES.
<br />
<br />PROGRAMA DO WORKSHOP
<br />Title: 360 Film, from theory to practice.
<br />
<br />In this workshop, we’ll look at the tools and methods available to create high quality 360 videos. We’ll look at the technical aspects of production, video and audio recording and different approaches each type of production can have. We will also go through every important detail for the post-production, presenting real life examples and technical details behind every shot.
<br /> With this, you’ll be able to get the know how with practical examples of “do’s and dont’s” to direct a 360º film of your own.
<br />
<br />Topics
<br />
<br />- Introduction to what is VR and where does 360º videos fit in
<br />- How to Capture 360 videos
<br />- What to have in mind when recording a film in 360
<br />- 360 Promotional vs Film
<br />-  Beaware of the audience
<br />- How to force the “view”
<br />- 360 Audio
<br />- Organisation and Workflow
<br />- Assemble the puzzle
<br />- Editing in the timeline
<br />- Sound Editing
<br />- VFX Magic
<br />- Delivery
<br /> 
<br />Practice
